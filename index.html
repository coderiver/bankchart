Find and compare credit cards in Australia

Whether you’re looking for low rate credit card, 0% balance transfer card deals, rewards points or more, Mozo can guide you through the credit card maze. Our database has all the latest interest rates and fees and covers hundreds of Australian credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard and American Express.

Our nifty comparison tools let you quickly filter and weigh up different cards according to the features you want, such as no annual fee or interest free days. Try our search tool and research credit cards Australia wide in seconds

If you have a credit card already you can also run it through our free credit card Switch & Save Calculator. It will tell you the best cards that will save you the most in interest and fees by switching.

The more comprehensive comparison site

Our credit card comparison tools let you filter and compare providers online according to the features you want and you can also read reviews from real customers around Australia to get the inside story on everything from hidden fees to customer service. Once you find the best credit card for you, our quick and convinient online credit card application makes getting your new card a breeze.

If you prefer to compare by card type you can find information here on Gold cards, Platinum cards, American Express, Mastercard credit cards and Visa credit cards.

Low interest cards

Before you begin your research, it helps to work out what sort of credit card user you are. If you often pay interest on your card, a low rate card will be best help you keep your debt under control.

The best balance transfer credit cards can really help you to pay off your debt faster, but be sure to check the interest rate that applies once the balance transfer period expires before your application.
